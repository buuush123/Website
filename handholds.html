Handholds

There are various amount of handholds and footholds in indoor rock climbing. Some handholds are not always grippable, either due to its shape or friction. There are many kinds that are used for your feet, and your hands. One of the hardest footholes is called the pancake. The pancakes are very hard to use because they are flat circles on the all for only your feet. My least favorite kinds of hand holds are the round fat handholds. It is difficult because its like holding on to a ball but squarish with smooth sides.
